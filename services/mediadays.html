Media Days

ONE SHOOT DAY.
A FULL SEASON OF CONTENT.

How your program looks on the outside is just as important as what happens on the field.

Running an athletic program at the highest level takes real commitment. Most coaches know that for recruits, parents, and the broader community, your media is the first impression, and it shapes how seriously your program gets taken before anyone ever steps foot in your facility.

But producing consistent, professional content takes time, equipment, and expertise most programs simply don't have on staff. A Colosseum Media Day gives your program everything it needs in a single shoot day, so your staff can stay focused on what matters most.

WHAT WE OFFER

Every program has different needs and a different budget. Whether you're a professional organization looking for a fully customized media production or a youth team wanting professional photos your athletes are proud of, we build around what fits your program.

Player Photos

Not your standard backdrop and smile setup. These are creative, professionally lit individual shots that players are genuinely excited to post. Built for social media, recruiting profiles, and anywhere your athletes need to show up looking their best.

Hype Videos

Cinematic team content that builds excitement around your program. Season openers, player features, and promotional videos your athletes actually want to share.

Custom Graphics

Game day graphics, final score templates, player features, and commitment announcements. Designed around your program's identity and ready to use all season.

Not sure what fits your program? Start with a discovery call and we'll figure it out together.

THE PACKAGES

ESSENTIALS

Professional studio-style player photos with clean backdrops and pro lighting. The foundation every program needs.

Best for: Programs establishing a consistent, professional visual standard.
PRO

Everything in Essentials plus a cinematic team hype video, custom graphics templates for game day, final scores, player features, and commitment announcements, plus position group and senior photos.

Packages are priced by roster size. Start with a discovery call and we'll find the right fit for your program and your budget.
