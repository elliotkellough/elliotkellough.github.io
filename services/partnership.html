03
Team Media Partnership

YOUR PROGRAM. COVERED ALL YEAR.
EVERY GAME, EVERY MOMENT, EVERY SEASON.

Your program has a culture worth showing.

The coaches who built it, the athletes who live it, and the community around it all deserve to see it represented properly. We embed with your organization as your dedicated media team, handling everything from game day coverage and player features to community events and off season content.

The result is a program that shows up consistently, looks elite every week, and builds the kind of online presence that attracts recruits, engages your community, and makes sponsors want to be part of what you're building.

WHY IT MATTERS

Sponsorship

Sponsors pay to be associated with programs that have attention. A program with inconsistent or low quality media has nothing compelling to sell a potential sponsor. A program with a professional, active, and engaged media presence is a different conversation entirely. We build the content infrastructure that makes your program attractive to the sponsors you want.

Recruiting

The first thing a recruit does when they're considering your program is look you up. What they find shapes their decision before you've ever had a conversation with them. A consistent, high quality media presence signals that your program is serious, well run, and worth their time.

WHAT THIS LOOKS LIKE

Every partnership is built around what your program needs. Here's what we can cover.

In Season
Game day highlight edits
Player of the game graphics (offense, defense, special teams)
Game day photo graphics
Player and coach features
Weekly social content and trending format videos
Commitment graphics for new signings
Sponsorship content and graphics
Year Round
