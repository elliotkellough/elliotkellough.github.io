Our Story

BUILT FOR
THE MOMENT.

2025 FOUNDED

NOT OBSERVERS.
ATHLETES.

We didn't come to Canadian sports media from the outside. We came from inside it. And that changes everything about how we work, what we make, and what we're building toward.

Daniel and Roan met playing football at the University of Calgary. They were teammates on the Dinos, competed at the highest level of university football in Canada, and shared the same standard — the kind that gets built in locker rooms and on practice fields, not in classrooms.

After football, both stayed in business. Daniel went into marketing, spent time at Red Bull learning how world-class brands build cultural relevance, and came away convinced that Canadian sports deserved that same level of creative investment. Roan learned how people make decisions, how to listen, and how to close.

"Canadian sport is full of personalities, stories, and moments the rest of the world hasn't seen yet. We're here to change that."

Colosseum was founded in April 2025 with one belief: that the best content comes from people who actually understand the world they're filming. Not observers. Not outsiders. People who lived it.

In our first year we produced campaigns for NFL-affiliated programs, multi-athlete brand productions with credible names from across Canadian sport, and media day productions that gave programs a full season of professional content in a single shoot day. We're just getting started.

Our Mission

TO BUILD THE STANDARD
FOR CANADIAN SPORTS MEDIA.

We exist to give Canadian sport the creative investment it deserves. Not generic highlight reels. Not stock footage with a logo slapped on top. Real, cinematic storytelling built by people who understand what's actually happening on the field.

Every project starts with the same question: what does this team, athlete, or brand actually deserve to look like? Then we build toward that standard, no matter what it takes to get there.

02 · Core Values

THE STANDARD
DOES NOT MOVE.

Eight values, shared like a locker room code. Every person who carries the Colosseum name carries these.

01 The Athlete Standard

This business runs like a championship team. Everyone earns their spot, everyone owns their role, and everyone is expected to perform at the highest level every single day. The best players play.

02 Win Together, Lose Together

We are one team. We own the wins and the losses together, hold each other accountable, and expect the best out of each other. Iron sharpens iron.

03 Do Your Job at the Highest Level

Every person on this team has a role. Own it completely. The details matter, the deadlines matter, the quality of your work matters. If your teammate needs help, you help them.

04 Craft First

The standard does not move. Every piece of content, every client interaction, every deliverable that carries the Colosseum name is held to the same bar. Worth watching, worth sharing, worth being proud of.

05 Personality with Respect

Colosseum is not a buttoned up company and we never try to be. We let our personalities show and bring energy to every interaction. What never changes is the respect we give to the people in the room. Every client, every athlete, every collaborator gets a positive experience from us. Full stop. We read the room. If someone is direct and commanding, we match that energy. If they are warm and relaxed, we meet them there. We do not complain. Nobody likes working with a complainer.

06 We Want to Be the Best

Our company culture is competitive and driven to be the best sports media company in the country. We celebrate that ambition internally, recruit people who share it, and measure everything against the highest possible standard.

07 Be the Most Prepared Person in the Room

Every meeting, every call, every shoot, every conversation where we are working with someone else: come prepared. Know the objective. Know the material. Know the people in the room. Come with an agenda, come with questions, and come with something worth contributing. This applies to client meetings, internal team meetings, creative sessions, and everything in between. Preparation is how we show respect for everyone's time and how we earn the right to lead the conversation.

08 One Step Above

In every interaction and deliverable we create for a client, our goal is to go one step beyond their expectation. The difference between a client who comes back and one who doesn't is almost always found in the small things that were better than anticipated. That is the standard we hold ourselves to on every project.
